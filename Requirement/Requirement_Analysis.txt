Functional Requirement
 - Passenger can update personal information.
 - Passenger can report any problems to the Bus company.
 - Passenger can check their ticket after buying.

 - Driver can do the attendance check.
 - Driver can view and register their working schedule weekly / daily.
 - Driver can check their salary based on their working hours.

 - Bus company can update / edit the information about their business.
 - Bus company can update / edit their discount programs / promotions.
 - Bus company can update / edit the ticket's price.
 - Bus company can receive the report of customer and view the working hour of drivers.
 - Bus company can view the daily / weekly / monthly / annual profits and sales.
...

Non-Functional Requirement
 - Passenger can buy ticket by entering pick-up location, destination and date.
